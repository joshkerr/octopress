Just Add Water: Household Cleaner in an Empty Bottle

Nov 2nd, 2010

Okay, this is really cool. Since 90% of the makeup of a common household cleaner is water, why not ship it without the water and let the consumer fill it with their own? I imagine this saves huge on shipping costs especially if these cleaners are being flown from somewhere. The product is appropriately called Replenish.
